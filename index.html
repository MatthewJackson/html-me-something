My Profession

I currently switched careers in October to a great company called Presto-X, a branch of Rentokil North America. It's in corporate pest control. I never thought I would end up enjoying a job where I'm outside in the cold but so far I really like it. I like jobs that challenge me and allow me to learn something new. In this case I wanted to learn a trade. It also gave me the ability to take this class. Which my previous job would not have let me do. I spend most of my days servicing warehouses but also places you don't think about. Such as restaurants, hospitals, and hotels. That's not to say they have issues but my job is to help prevent pest issues, not react to them. So far I've really enjoyed it.
